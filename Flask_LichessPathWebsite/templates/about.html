About Page

Hi, my name is Daniel and i tried to find the path from me to magnus carlsen, who is world chess champion.
what is a path?
a path is a chain of wins, from me to magnus that every link of the chain is a game won.

Example:
sheynan -> renkum79 -> arashtash1976 -> isitajoke -> Zhalmakhanov_R -> DrNykterstein sheynan won renkum79, who won arashtash1976, who won isitajoke, who won Zhalmakhanov_R, who won DrNykterstein. (magnus carlsen)

The Data Set

I parsed the lichess data base (can be found here) and added about 550,000 users. and about 30 million games. each player has a 'Distance' property, that indicates how many degrees are from you to Magnus.
